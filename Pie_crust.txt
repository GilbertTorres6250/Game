Recipe: Pie crust
Ingredients:
1 tbsp of sugar
1 tsp of salt
2 cups of flour
1cup of water
1 1/2 sticks of butter
Directions:
Mix dry ingrediants in seperate bowl.
Mix dry ingrediants and butter in mixer.
Slowly add water until doughy.
Cool pie dust in parchment paper until chilled.

Unravel and roll out into pie pan. Place parchment over and cover in beans or other heavy material.
Place pan in over for a couple minutes, remove and place pie filling. Bake on 350 for 45 minutes. Yay Pie
