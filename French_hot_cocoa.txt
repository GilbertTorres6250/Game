Recipe: French hot cocoa
Ingredients:
300g Chocolate(2 150g bars)
350g whole milk
200g half & half
4 teaspoons of powdered sugar
pinch of salt
whipped cream(topping)
chocolate shavings(topping)
Directions:
Chop 2 chocolate bars

in a pot put 350g of whole milk with 200g of half & half, the chopped chocolate, and 4 teaspoons of powdered sugar

bring to a simmer and leave it at a simmer until all the chocolate melts then serve with toppings
